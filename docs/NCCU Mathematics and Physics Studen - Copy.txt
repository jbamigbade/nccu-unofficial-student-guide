General Mathematics Student Advice

Students recommend:
- Attending class regularly.
- Using tutoring services when needed.
- Forming study groups.
- Starting homework early.
- Visiting office hours.
- Reviewing notes weekly.
- Practicing mathematics daily.
- Keeping track of assignment deadlines.
- Preparing for exams several weeks in advance.
- Using campus academic support resources.
